Baltimore Avenue and Knox

The block of Baltimore Avenue/Route One between Lehigh and Knox Road is one of the more flood-prone of the business community. Since it is state property, efforts must be coordinated with the Maryland State Highway Administration. The area is a pipeshed that captures much of downhill flow from streets and parking lots above the storm drains, and the pipe size is not large enough to handle the flow during significant storm events.
